Multiphase flow simulator

1-phase flow simulator

Limitations:

  • Uniform 1-dimensional grid
  • Constant timesteps
  • Constant permeability
  • Constant porosity
  • Constant viscosity
  • Constant small compressibility
  • The simplest explicit finite difference scheme
